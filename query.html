❓ Frequently Asked

What are the monthly fees? ›
Fees range from ₹800–₹1,200/month per subject depending on the batch level. Both subjects together get a 10% discount.
How is my child placed in a batch? ›
We conduct a free 20-minute diagnostic test to identify your child's level and place them in Foundation, Regular, or Advanced batch accordingly.
Is there a free trial? ›
Yes! First week of classes is completely free with no commitment. Try it and decide.
Can my child switch batches? ›
Yes. Students who improve significantly are moved to a higher batch after monthly assessment results.
What if my child misses a class? ›
Notes are shared on WhatsApp. Missed topics can be covered in Sunday doubt sessions. No student is left behind.
